About Us

Infratop, at the heart of your projects

A new player that will support your projects from start to finish.

Drawing from their experience in construction machinery and responding to market demands, three of Altitop's founders - Frédéric and Mathieu Naessens, along with Mehdi Palumbo - decided to create INFRATOP.

Infratop's mission is to combine local customer service with high-end equipment incorporating the latest technologies that will help drive the ecological transition of your construction sites.

Altitop is recognized in the industry for its service and quality. Rest assured that you will be well supported by a qualified INFRA team ensuring optimal equipment and service. Your trust and increasingly specific demands in your projects drive us to continuously move forward.
